# 💎 Subscriber Role

**Access Level:** PAID
**Purpose:** Premium users with access to advanced reading tools.

## Access Profile

The Subscriber role is for users who want to go deeper into the collection. It unlocks advanced metadata (word counts, durations), improved navigation (directories), and performance-focused reading tools (speed reader, ORP).

## Strategic Purpose

The Subscriber role is the primary monetization vector. It targets power-users, researchers (Culture Explorers, Creatives), and those who value the "OS for stories" vision over just a basic reader.
